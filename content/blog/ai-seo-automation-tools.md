## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### 1. Over-reliance on AI
While AI tools are powerful, they should supplement, not replace, human expertise:

- Review AI recommendations critically
- Maintain brand voice and style
- Ensure content quality and accuracy
- Consider user intent and experience

### 2. Ignoring Technical SEO
AI tools primarily focus on content optimization. Don't neglect technical aspects:

- Site speed optimization
- Mobile responsiveness
- Schema markup implementation
- Core Web Vitals improvement

### 3. Inconsistent Implementation
Maintain consistency in your AI SEO strategy:

- Regular tool usage and monitoring
- Consistent content optimization
- Ongoing performance analysis
- Continuous strategy refinement
